Facts on Camels

1. Camels can go upto 5 days without drinking water.
2. Camels have humps which store fat, which is nutrition for the camel when it has no food.
3. Camels have thick eyelashes that keep sand out of their eyes.

Facts on Chamaleons

1. Chamaleons can change their skin colour to match their surroundings. They do this to hide from predators.
2. Half of the known Chamaleon speices live in Madagascar.
3. Unlike other lizards, Chamaleons cannot regrow their tails.
